Pedestrian crossing

This example is hard, because the monocular sensor image may look indistinguishable from a 3D object. The severity of the example is relatively low, bacause at worst an autonomous vehicle will stop and block the traffic.

An exotic animal crossing a road

This example is hard, because of the exotic shape of an animal. The severity of this example is low, because an animal is large and would be easily registered by multiple sensors of an autonomous vehicle.
